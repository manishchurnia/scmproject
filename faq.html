    • What is BMI Calculator and how does it work?

    A BMI calculator is a tool that estimates body fat percentage and risk of certain diseases based on a person's height and weight.

    BMI is calculated by dividing weight by height squared. For example: to calculate BMI in pounds and inches, you can multiply your weight by 703, then divide that number by your height in inches twice.
